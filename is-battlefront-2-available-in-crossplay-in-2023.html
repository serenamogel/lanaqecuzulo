Is Battlefront 2 Available in Crossplay in 2023?

August 2024 · 3 minute read

Are you looking forward to the year 2023, when Battlefront 2 could finally be a fully cross-platform game? Crossplay has been a long-time request from gamers hoping for their console and PC friends to join in on their gaming sessions. With rumors swirling about new titles offering the feature, it begs the question: is Battlefront 2 one of them?

This blog post takes an analytical look at what it would take for crossplay to come to this beloved sci-fi shooter by 2023 and whether or not it’s feasible. So read on if you’re interested in taking your Battlefront experience further with even more intense battlefield warfare!

Overview of Star Wars Battlefront 2 and what Crossplay Means in Gaming.

Star Wars Battlefront 2 is a popular multiplayer action shooter game that was released in 2017. The game has been gathering many players both on PC and console platforms, making it a great option for gamers looking to get into the Star Wars universe.

Recently, the developers of Star Wars Battlefront 2 announced they were introducing crossplay, which allows gamers to play against each other regardless of their platform. This means that if you’re playing on Xbox One, you can now compete with players from PlayStation 4 or PC.

Crossplay opens up the game to a much wider audience and gives players more options when it comes to choosing who they want to battle against.

It also brings with it an improved matchmaking system, allowing players to find an appropriate match faster than ever before. With these new features, Star Wars Battlefront 2 is poised to become one of the most popular multiplayer games around.

Are there any chances that Battlefront will be available in crossplay Soon

As it stands, Battlefront 2 has yet to include crossplay as a feature, which means players on PlayStation can only compete with other PS4 gamers.

But there could be an interesting development in this regard, as Electronic Arts just introduced the concept of crossplay into their recently released game Need for Speed Heat.

This marks the first time EA has added such a feature in one of their titles and suggests that they might slowly start introducing cross-play into more of their games. While nothing has been set in stone yet, fans may be hopeful that FIFA 21 could be among the first to benefit from this change.

While many gamers are still waiting for an announcement from EA about whether or not crossplay will be available in Battlefront 2, it seems unlikely that the feature will be added to the game. However, given the popularity of crossplay in other games, there is always the possibility that EA could change its mind and add support for crossplay down the line. For now, though, gamers who want to play with their friends on different platforms will have to look elsewhere.
