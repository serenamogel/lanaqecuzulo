Gut Check! Rhett &amp; Link Try Every Talenti Gelato Flavor

May 2024 · 2 minute read

On today’s episode of Good Mythical Summer, Rhett & Link dug their spoons into the world of Talenti Gelato to find out which flavor is the dreamiest and creamiest. Just how do these frozen desserts stack up against one another? The guys tried almost 20 different flavors, but these are the five that they think are worthy of your freezer. (Spoiler: Their favorite flavor is the Sporked crew’s favorite flavor, too!)

Madagascan Vanilla Bean (Rhett: 70 | Link: 76) 

“I think vanilla really shines in a gelato,” Link said as he began his evaluation. Rhett responded, “It’s so simple, it’s a little bit elegant, though.” This one was definitely a cut above the rest in terms of flavor for the pair, but only Link seemed to be majorly impressed. Much respect was given to this Talenti flavor. 

Belgian Chocolate (Rhett: 78 | Link: 68)

This was the first flavor they tasted, and they were blown away by how smooth Talenti is overall. This flavor didn’t crack the top three, but it would definitely would satisfy a chocolate craving. 

Chocolate Peanut Butter Cup (Rhett: 79 | Link: 74) 

When Link tasted this his first words were, “Spot on.” Rhett asked for a little bit more. This gelato was the first of the Gut Check to really wow the pair (not surprisingly given their love of peanut butter). They both noted just how much Talenti nailed the peanut butter flavor; Link especially loved the ribbons of peanut butter that wend their way through the pint.

Coffee Chocolate Chip (Rhett: 80 | Link: 75)

 “That’s really nice,” Rhett said, calling this flavor of Talenti Gelato “unexpectedly good.” Both Rhett & Link loved how the coffee flavor balanced with the sweetness and cream of the gelato. This gelato was only two points away from tying for the top spot.

Raspberry Cheesecake (Rhett: 80 | Link: 77)

Given Rhett & Link’s history with raspberry cheesecake (they love it), it’s no major surprise that this gelato came out on top. “It just tastes like a liquified cheesecake,” Rhett said; Link added, “That’s beautiful.” Raspberry Cheesecake Talenti Gelato reigns supreme!

Rhett’s Favorite Flavor: Raspberry Cheesecake | Coffee Chocolate Chip.

Link’s Favorite Flavor: Raspberry Cheesecake

The Worsts: Caramel Cookie Crunch & Caramel Gingerbread

Rhett & Link Also Tried:

Black Raspberry Chocolate Chip, Chocolate Chip Cookie Dough, Double Dark Chocolate, Dairy-Free Double Cookie Crunch, Vanilla Caramel Swirl, Sea Salt Caramel, Dairy-Free Caramel Toffee Crunch, Caribbean Coconut, Mediterranean Mint, Pacific Coast Pistachio, Southern Butter Pecan
