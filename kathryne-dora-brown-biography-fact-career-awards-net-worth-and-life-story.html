Kathryne Dora Brown (born February 10, 1971) is an American actress.

Personal

Born in Los Angeles, California, she is the daughter of actor Georg Stanford Brown and actress Tyne Daly. Her uncle is actor Tim Daly.

Career

Brown has appeared in many television movies and series as well as film. In addition, she has appeared on the stage. She made her stage debut at the Mark Taper Forum (Los Angeles, California) in Changes of Heart in 1996.
