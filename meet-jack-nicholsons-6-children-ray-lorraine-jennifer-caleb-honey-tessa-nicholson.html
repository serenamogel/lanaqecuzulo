Meet Jack Nicholson’s 6 children Ray, Lorraine, Jennifer, Caleb, Honey & Tessa Nicholson

Ray, Lorraine, Jennifer, Caleb, Honey, and Tessa Nicholson are celebrity kids and celebrities on their own who happen to be the children of an American retired actor and filmmaker, Jack Nicholson. The six children were born to Nicholson and five women.

Jennifer Nicholson was an actress who had a successful Hollywood career. She worked on a variety of projects during her early entertainment career, including “The Two Jakes” (1990) starring Jack Nicholson and “Hoffa” (1992) starring her father. She happens to be the first and only child of Jack Nicholson and his ex-wife, Sandra Knight. She was born to them on September 13, 1963, in Los Angeles, California, United States, hence, she is 60 years old.

Caleb Goddard is an actor, known for Guilty as Charged (1991) and The Slap Maxwell Story (1987). He is the only child of Jack Nicholson and Susan Anspach. He was born to his parents on September 26, 1970, in Los Angeles, California, USA, hence, he is 52 years old.

Honey Hollman is an actress, known for Empire North (2010) and Go’ aften Danmark (2002). She is the only child of her parents – Jack Nicholson and Winnie Hollman. She was born to them on January 26, 1982, in Copenhagen, Denmark, hence, she is 42 years old.

Lorraine Broussard Nicholson is an American actress and director known for playing Alana Blanchard in the biographical film Soul Surfer. She is the daughter of Jack Nicholson and Rebecca Broussard.  She happens to be the oldest of two children of her parents, hence, she has a full younger brother. She was born to her parents on April 16, 1990, in Los Angeles, California, United States, hence, she is 33 years old.

Ray Nicholson is an American actor who appeared opposite Diane Kruger in the Neil LaBute film Out of the Blue. On television, he appeared in the 2021 Amazon Prime Video series Panic. He is the youngest of two children of his parents Jack Nicholson and Rebecca Broussard. He was born to his parents on February 20, 1992, in Los Angeles, California, United States, hence, he is 31 years old.

Tessa Gourin is an actress, known for Stranger’s Arms (2018), Untitled True Crime Project, and Stage Play (2021). She is the only child of her parents – Jack Nicholson and Jeannine Gourin. She happens to be the youngest of six children of her father. She was born to her parents on August 15, 1994, in New York City, New York, USA, hence, she is 29 years old.
