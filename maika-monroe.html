An American actress and a professional kiteboarder Maika Monroe is known for her breakthrough horror film named ‘It Follows’ which was released in the year 2014. She has won several awards and also earned an Empire Awards nomination.

She has been recognized for her outstanding work and performances in the horror genre. She is known for her work in the thriller ‘The Guest’ which was released in the year 2014.

Career

Maika Monroe has acted in the most popular films and television series including the sci-fi thriller Tau (2018) and the psychological thriller Greta (2018).

Maika Monroe started her acting journey with the drama film named ‘At Any Price’ in 2012. Apart from this, she worked in the horror drama film named ‘Labor Day’ which was released in the year 2013.

Among other acting credits, Monroe became famous for appearing in the action film Independence Day: Resurgence (2016), the neo-noir coming-of-age film Hot Summer Nights (2017), and many more that are prominent because of her performances.

Additionally, Maika Monroe has acted in the comedy-drama film After Everything (2018), the black comedy film Villains (2019), and many others.

Achievements

The actress has won innumerable awards and nominations including Home Media Magazine Awards,   Hamptons International Film Festival, Fright Meter Awards, iHorror Awards, Empire Awards, Fangoria Chainsaw Awards, and many others.
