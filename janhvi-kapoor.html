Janhvi Kapoor is a film actress, hailing from India and born to veteran, late actress Sridevi and a filmmaker Bony Kapoor. She was born on 6th March 1997, India where she raised and grew up along with her younger sister, Khushi Kapoor. Also, she shares a strong bond with her half-siblings, Arjun Kapoor, and Anshula Kapoor. As she has strong roots in the Bollywood film industry, Janhvi Kapoor chooses to be an actress and after completing her studies, she joined the film industry. While she is mainly known for her debut movie “Dharak” for which she earned huge acclaim.

Career

Janhvi Kapoor started her career with a debut movie “Dharak” which was released in 2018 for which she won the award. The film was directorial of Shahshank Kaitan which was a remake of a Marathi movie “Sairat”. Later, in the same year, Janhvi Kapoor got selected as a brand ambassador for a cosmetics brand Nykaa.

In 2020, she made an appearance in the Netflix horror anthology movie named “Ghost Stories” which was a segment of Zoya Akhtar. Moreover, Janhvi Kapoor appeared in the movie “Gunjan Saxena: The Kargil Girl” and also appeared in the comedy-horror movie “Roohi Afzana” in which she worked alongside Rajkumar Rao. Also, she is going to share the screen space with Kartik Aaryan and Laksh Lalwani in the movie “Dostana 2” and also gets featured in Karan Johar’s directorial “Takht”. However, the emerging actress has accomplished her goals and became the most popular star in the Hindi cinema in a brief career.

Achievements

Janhvi Kapoor’s Dharak got commercial success and she won the Zee Cine Award for the Best Female Debut.
