Dillon Danis' racy 'Logan you wild for this' tweet fires more shots at Paul

June 2024 · 2 minute read

Dillon Danis is still trolling Logan Paul ahead of their fight and the latest post is a raunchy image captioned: “Logan you wild for this.”

The pair are getting in the ring on October 14 at Manchester’s AO Arena on the undercard of the much-anticipated Tommy Fury vs KSI fight.

‘Logan you wild for this’

Dillon Danis took to Twitter on Wednesday (September 6) to share a raunchy image that he claims is of Logan Paul, although it’s likely to be fake.

The X-rated picture shows a man standing up as someone bends down in front of him. A monkey emoji and black scribble cover the intimate parts.

He added the caption: “Logan you wild for this.” The YouTuber turned-boxer hasn’t responded to the image when writing this.

Fans say it ‘can’t be real’

Fans are really confused in the comments, with one person writing “Is this Logan?” and another adding: “Wait is this real?”

At first glance, the man does resemble Paul, but it’s important to remember that the image is likely to be photoshopped.

Some think the other person looks like KSI, but it could very well be fabricated in an attempt to troll the YouTuber before the fight.

One fan said the image “can’t be real,” while another added “This gotta be fake” and a third wrote: “Definitely photoshop.”

Nina Agdal sues Dillon Danis

Dillon has been firing shots at Logan and his girlfriend Nina Agdal for weeks now and the Danish model has had enough.

The 31-year-old has sued the MMA star, TMZ Sports reveals – and filed for a restraining order following the horrific trolling.

She reportedly filed the documents on Wednesday (September 6), claiming Danis has posted “despicable” things about her more than 250 times.

Agdal says she’s suffered “humiliation, emotional distress, and reputational harm” and claims he shared a sexually explicit image of her.

“Nina Agdal has filed a massive lawsuit against me. She filed a restraining order against me and is seeking prison time, so the fight is in jeopardy if I’m in jail,” the fighter tweeted in response.

“This is actually wild but I won’t stop f*** the system come get me. Logan Paul is a dead man walking.”
