Paula Patton is an American actress. She was born on December 5, 1975, in Los Angeles, California, United States. She is the daughter of Charles Patton and Joyce Patton.

Paula joined Hamilton High School. Later, she attended the University of California, Berkeley, and just after one year she transferred to the University of Southern California’s film school. She later graduated from film school.

Paula began her career with the 2005 romantic comedy film Hitch starred Will Smith and Eva Mendes. Just after the year, she appeared in the musical film Idlewild along with Andre Benjamin and Big Boi. Paula has been a regular member of the heavy hit ABC drama series, Somewhere Between. Her recent ventured incle the Perfect Match, The Do-Over, and Warcraft.
